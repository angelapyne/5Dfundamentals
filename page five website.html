Hello!

My name is Angela Pyne and I am a third year Graphic Design student at Northeastern University. I began my work as a designer six years ago when I took my first art class at Walpole High School. Since then I have gone on to freelancing for a few local businesses and groups.



Some projects I’ve worked on include:

- The Walpole Town Forest Committee
- GMC Culinary Kitchen
- The Iron Chef Junior competition
- Austine School for the Deaf Historical Committee


As a budding graphic designer I am ​always eager to learn and expand my work. I love to experiment with new and different styles, and try to challenge myself on every new project I begin. I enjoy incorporating my fun and friendly personality into the independent projects I do, as well as my Fine Art.
